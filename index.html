The iPhone 17 introduces a sleek new design with a 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display that supports ProMotion up to 120 Hz and an Always-On feature. It runs on the powerful A19 chip, offering faster performance and improved efficiency. The phone includes dual 48-megapixel rear cameras and an 18-megapixel front camera, providing excellent photo and video quality. With iOS 26, larger base storage starting at 256 GB, and a lightweight aluminum body, the iPhone 17 delivers premium features once reserved for “Pro” models.

The thinnest iPhone ever made.

The iPhone Air’s ultra-thin design makes it incredibly lightweight and comfortable to carry anywhere. Despite its slim build, it delivers impressive performance and a premium feel that enhances everyday use.

Available in 4 different colors.

Cloud White

Clean and minimalist, ideal for a simple yet elegant appearance.

Light Gold

A warm, luxurious hue that gives the phone a refined and high-end aesthetic.

Sky Blue

A soft, cool-toned blue that feels calm and modern.

Space Black

A timeless, classic shade that gives the phone a sleek and professional look.
