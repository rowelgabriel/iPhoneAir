The iPhone 17 introduces a sleek new design with a 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display that supports ProMotion up to 120 Hz and an Always-On feature. It runs on the powerful A19 chip, offering faster performance and improved efficiency. The phone includes dual 48-megapixel rear cameras and an 18-megapixel front camera, providing excellent photo and video quality. With iOS 26, larger base storage starting at 256 GB, and a lightweight aluminum body, the iPhone 17 delivers premium features once reserved for “Pro” models.
